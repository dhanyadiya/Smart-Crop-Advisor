Rice Crop Information

Rice is one of the most important staple foods for a large part of the world's population, especially in Asia and Africa. Here are some key details about rice farming:

Soil Type: Clayey soil with good water retention.

Temperature Range: 20°C - 35°C (Optimum 25°C).

Rainfall: Requires 100-200 cm of rainfall annually.

Water Requirement: Rice fields must remain flooded for optimal growth.

Planting Season: Usually planted during the monsoon season (June to November).

Common Varieties: Basmati, Jasmine, Japonica, and more.
